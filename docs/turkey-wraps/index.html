What's Cookin'

Reviews of food from my kitchen and the grocery store

Turkey Bacon Lettuce Tomato Avocado Wraps

When I do see a clock that shows it's noon, my stomach growls with zeal of a baboon. I hurry t'ward the kitchen pantry quick. What can be mixed? I do arithmetic.

To curb hunger, there is one choice: a wrap. Multigrain, local! What could be better? Inside: turkey, bacon, lettuce, and more. All sealed via searing, wrapped up tight.

I savor every bite, no time to nap. This certainly will be a trend-setter. Beware: the mustard leaks, it's on the floor. Not long, it's gone. Like life, wraps are finite.
